Research from my **AI Model Research Internship at Samsung Electronics** (Summer 2025) on
**merging multimodal large language models**.

**Contributions.**
- Designed **layer-wise merge strategies** that improved language (LLM) benchmark scores
  while keeping multimodal (LMM) performance above target (>64).
- Built a **scalable safetensors merging pipeline** supporting multiple models and
  efficient weight-integration workflows.
- Evaluated merged models with LLMeval and LMMeval, confirming language gains without
  multimodal regression.

*Code is proprietary to Samsung Electronics and not publicly available.*
