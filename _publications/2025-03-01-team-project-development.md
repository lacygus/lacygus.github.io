Analyzing multi-session interdisciplinary team discussions to understand how project ideas
evolve from exploration to outcomes. The work models insider–outsider roles, conversational
centrality, and turn-taking dynamics to capture evolving interaction structures, and
quantifies functional patterns and topic convergence using intent distributions,
transition matrices, and entropy measures.

*Working paper (in progress).*
